info about us

Welcome to Page Turner, a site tailored to meet your book-finding needs. Page Turner is the work of a small group of developers committed to bringing you a convenient place to browse and buy books. We unite to provide an easy-to-use, minimalist site for the book lover in all of us.

how to use

To use our site, simply type what you would like to search and hit enter. A page will show your results, along with a more advanced tool to help you filter and refine your search. When you find a book that you like, click on its image and you will see more information about the book. From that page, you can follow a link to buy the book or Tweet about it! Anytime you wish to return to the home page, hit the Page Turner logo in your navigation bar.
